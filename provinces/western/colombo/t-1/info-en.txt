This temple is located on the Mahamegarama Road, about half a kilometer from Maharagama in the Colombo District of the Western Province. Venerable Ankumbure Amaravansa Thera has been the incumbent of this temple since 1992, after the passing of the venerable Nayaka Thero. The Dhamma School, which was established in 1953, is dedicated to the Dhamma education of Buddhist children in the area. About four hundred children are currently studying in this Buddhist institution, known as Sri Suganapala Dhamma School. Special Buddhist programs held every Poya Day include Dhamma sermons, Bodhi Pooja, Pahan Pooja, and many other auspicious events. This Buddhist shrine has become popular with all Buddhists living in the area.
